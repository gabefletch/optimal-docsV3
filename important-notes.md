## About Different Optimal Versions

**Optimal on Readymag (OoR)**

v10.0 and above of Optimal are run on the Readymag site engine and are referred to on Optimal Docs as Optimal on Readymag or OoR.&#x20;

**Optimal on Carrd (OoC)**

v9.3.1.6 and below of Optimal are run on the Carrd site engine and are referred to on Optimal Docs as Optimal on Carrd or OoC.&#x20;
